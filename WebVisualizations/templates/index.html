Summary: Latitude vs. X

This project was focused on analyzing weather patterns as you get closer to the equator using data, from more than 500 cities, that was pulled from OpenWeatherMap API.

Matplotlib was then used to plot weather factors in relation to the latitude of the varius cities. These factors included: temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed. The source data, and the visualizations created from the data, are displayed, along with explanations of any noticeable trends or correlations.
